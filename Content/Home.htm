Frequently Asked Questions

When can I visit the castle?

You can visit the castle on Tuesdays: from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

How long does the Pszczyna castle tour last?

The guided tour lasts one hour for a tour of the interiors,

two hours for a tour of the interiors and other

exhibitions.

What is worth visiting in Pszczyna?

There are many places worth visiting, including:

  • the park,
  • the castle,
  • the Open-Air museum,
  • the museum of the Silesian press.
