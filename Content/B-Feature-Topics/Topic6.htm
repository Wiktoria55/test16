Tearoom „Daisy”

While in the park, we will see one of the most picturesque and romantic places - the Pszczyna tearoom. It is an island with a tea pavilion that was built in the mid-19th century. In those days, tt was one of the favorite places of Princess Daisy, who at that time had to reach the island by boat. Currently, however, you can go there by walking over bridges. The island offers a beautiful view of the castle. In the evening the extraordinary décor and lighting create a magical atmosphere.
