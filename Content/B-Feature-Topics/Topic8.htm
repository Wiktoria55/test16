The European Bison Show Farm

The history of the appearance of bisons in Pszczyna is very long. It all started in 1865, when Prince John Henry XI Hochberg made an exchange with the Russian Tsar Alexander, receiving 4 bisons for 20 deer. It was then that the bison became a symbol of Pszczyna ancan be admired to this day in a farm located in the park. Every visitor can see the famous Pszczyna bisons in their natural habitat, as well as other animal species, such as:

  • mouflons,
  • deer,
  • peacocks,
  • ducks.

You can observe the animals from the viewing platform, preferably during feeding times. To learn more about this place, press here.
